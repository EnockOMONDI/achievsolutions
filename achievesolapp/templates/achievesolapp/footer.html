About Us

Achieve Solutions is a training and personal development outfit established in the republic of Kenya in the year 2008. Our services include training, motivational talks, life coaching, business coaching, theme based workshops, seminars and conferences, and team – building services that take a fresh look when we are involved. The Achieve Solutions goal is to motivate, inspire, empower and transform lives through provision of quality services that meet the needs of individuals, teams, and organizations. Achieve Solutions aspires to use its customized services to share knowledge for continuous learning, build skills and supplement information for personal improvement & growth, for better results, productive & cohesive teams, organization success, sustainable livelihoods, thriving businesses and empowered communities. Achieve Solutions is a ‘people factory’ of mindset change throughout Africa. At Achieve Solutions, we invest in people.
