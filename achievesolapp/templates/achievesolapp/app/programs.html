The program is designed to instil academic and moral discipline, encourage hard work and promote education. The program is set to drive learners to achieve academic excellence and become high performing individuals equipped for success. We encourage small group discussions and interactive sessions with facilitators. We engage fully with the students offering feedback sessions and access to a pool of experts. We remain committed to investing in students by providing them an opportunity to grow, learn, unlearn and relearn for purposes of producing responsible leaders of today and tomorrow.
